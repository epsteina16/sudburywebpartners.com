How long will it take to build my website?

That depends on how complex of a project it is. Small sites can take anywhere from 1-3 months and larger sites can take up to 4 months.

What tools do you use to build websites?

We use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to design our sites. On the coding side, we use HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript/JQuery on the front-end, and Python and PHP on the back-end.

Do you build mobile friendly sites?

Yes! All of our sites are built with responsive design, made to fit all devices, using HTML5 and CSS3.

Do you offer any content management systems?

Yes. We can build your site in Wordpress. We prefer to use custom code, as it allows for more customization and specialization, but we will be happy to build a site using CMS if it fits your needs.

Do you provide Web Hosting?

We do not provide web hosting, but will help set up your hosting account with a trusted provider. Web hosting can range from free, for small sites with not much traffic, to $10/month for larger sites.

Can I get an email account with my website?

Yes. We will set up email through a hosting provider.

What is Search Engine Optimization?

Search Engine Optimization, or SEO, is the use of a variety of tools to help improve a website's search engine rankings.

Do I need SEO?

Yes. SEO is needed in order to be found with search engines like Google and Bing.
